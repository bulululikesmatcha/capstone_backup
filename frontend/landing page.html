Tariff Rates

Volumetric charges only — billed based on cubic meters (m³) consumed per month.

Monthly Volumetric Charges

Commercial
Consumption Block (m³) Rate per m³ Description
0 – 10 m³ ₱12.00 Minimum block
11 – 20 m³ ₱16.00 Second block
21 – 30 m³ ₱20.00 Third block
31 – 40 m³ ₱24.00 Fourth block
41 m³ and above ₱28.00 Excess block

Only volumetric charges apply. No additional fees or VAT are added to the billed amount per POWASA policy.
